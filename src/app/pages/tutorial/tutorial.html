Welcome to Workout Planner

The Muscle Madness Workout Planner is a companison app to the people who are looking to get in shape and build muscle. This app will help you plan your workouts and give instructions on how to perform them.

What is Ionic?

In the workouts screen, you can view all the workouts that are available to you. You can also view the details of each workout by tapping on them. There are literally hundreds of workouts to choose from. If that is too much for you, you can also filter the workouts by muscle group, experience leve, available equipment....

View Workouts

The workout planner is where you can quickly view which routine you have to follow in a week. The planner uses customized AI engine to generate the best workout plan for you based on your goals, experience level, and available equipment. You can also customize the plan to your liking.
